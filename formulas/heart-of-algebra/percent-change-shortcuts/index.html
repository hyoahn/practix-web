Standard Percent Change

\[ \frac{\text{New} - \text{Old}}{\text{Old}} \times 100 \]

The universal formula for calculating percentage increase or decrease without confusion.

üìù Example: Basic Change

Price goes from $80 to $100. % Change?

\(\frac{100 - 80}{80} = \frac{20}{80} = 0.25 \rightarrow 25\%\)

Result: 25% Increase.
