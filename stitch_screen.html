General Form of a Circle

Find the radius of the circle:

x² + y² + 4x - 6y - 3 = 0
lightbulb

Quick Strategy

On Desmos, type the equation exactly as shown. Locate the center (-2, 3) and use the distance tool or simply count grid units to find the radius.

Formula Check: r = √(h² + k² - F) = √((-2)² + 3² - (-3)) = √(4 + 9 + 3) = √16 = 4
