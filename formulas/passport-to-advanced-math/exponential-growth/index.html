Growth & Decay

\[ y = a(1 \pm r)^t \]

The standard model for any percentage-based change over time. \(a\) is initial, \(r\) is rate, \(t\) is time.

"A value increases by 5% every year." ‚Üí Multiplier is \(1.05\).
"A value decreases by 7% every week." ‚Üí Multiplier is \(0.93\).
