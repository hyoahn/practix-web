Parabola Roots

Forget the Quadratic Formula. Find the answers instantly with Desmos.

Practice Challenge Finding Solutions

What are the solutions to the equation \(2x^2 - 8x - 10 = 0\)?

(A) \(x = -1\) and \(x = 5\)
(B) \(x = 1\) and \(x = -5\)
(C) \(x = 2\) and \(x = -10\)
(D) \(x = -2\) and \(x = 10\)
Click to see the "Desmos Speed-Run"

Don't solve algebraically.

1. Type y = 2x^2 - 8x - 10 into Desmos.

2. Look at where the parabola crosses the x-axis (the x-intercepts).

3. Click the gray intersection dots!

Desmos shows (-1, 0) and (5, 0) instantly. The solutions are \(x = -1\) and \(x = 5\). The answer is (A).
