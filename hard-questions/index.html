5. The Parameter Puzzle

ðŸš© The Trap

Plugging in numbers for \(x\) and \(k\) and hoping to find a pattern. This is a purely conceptual question.

âœ… The Practix Shortcut

Exactly one solution means the Discriminant is ZERO (\(b^2 - 4ac = 0\)).

\[ k^2 - 4(1)(9) = 0 \] \[ k^2 = 36 \] \[ k = 6 \text{ (since } k > 0) \]

Answer: 6. Master the discriminant, and these "hard" questions become free points.

6. The Standard Deviation Deception

ðŸš© The Trap

âœ… The Practix Shortcut

8. The Imaginary Illusion

ðŸš© The Trap

âœ… The Practix Shortcut

\[ \frac{(3+i)(2+i)}{(2-i)(2+i)} = \frac{6+3i+2i+i^2}{4-i^2} = \frac{5+5i}{5} = 1 + i \]

10. The Pizza Slice Puzzle
